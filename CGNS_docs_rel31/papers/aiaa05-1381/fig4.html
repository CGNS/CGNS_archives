This is a plot of wall time in seconds vs number of processors. There is one curve, for the 417 MB dataset. The curve starts at a wall time of about 13 for 1 processor, dips down to about 9 for 4 processors, climbs to 16 for 8 processors, then steadily decreases to about 4 for 50 or more processors.
