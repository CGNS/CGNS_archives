This figure illustrates how geometric entities and mesh regions may be associated in CGNS. On the right is a box labeled "Geometry Data" representing a geometric entity like a wing surface, or nacelle. It's connected by an arrow, pointing in both directions, to a box labeled "Intermediate Object / CFD Family", which is similarly connected to a box labeled "Mesh Entities" representing the regions of the computational mesh that correspond to the geometric entity.
