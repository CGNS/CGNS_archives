This diagram shows the structure of a GridConnectivity_t node and its children.

At the top is the GridConnectivity_t node. Below GridConnectivity_t are the following nodes.

  • IndexArray_t (CellListDonor)
  • Descriptor_t (text)
  • GridConnectivityProperty_t
  • GridConnectivityType_t (connectivity type)
  • GridLocation_t (location)
  • DataArray_t (InterpolantsDonor)
  • Ordinal_t (ordinal number)
  • IndexArray_t (PointList)
  • IndexArray_t (PointListDonor)
  • IndexRange_t (PointRange)
  • UserDefinedData_t
