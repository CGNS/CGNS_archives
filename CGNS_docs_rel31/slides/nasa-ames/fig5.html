This diagram shows the structure of a DataArray_t node and its children.

At the top is the DataArray_t node, containing data values. Below DataArray_t are the following nodes.

  • DataConversion_t (conversion factors)
  • DataClass_t (data class)
  • Descriptor_t (text)
  • DimensionalUnits_t (units)
  • DimensionalExponents_t (exponents)
