This figure shows some of the unstructured grid elements supported by CGNS, as follows.

  • TRI_3 - Two-dimensional triangular element, with three nodes located at the three geometric corners of the triangle.
  • QUAD_4 - Two-dimensional quadrilateral element, with four nodes located at the four geometric corners of the quadrangle.
  • TETRA_4 - Three-dimensional tetrahedral element, with four nodes located at the four geometric corners of the tetrahedron.
  • PYRA_5 - Three-dimensional pyramid element, with five nodes located at the five geometric corners of the pyramid.
  • PENTA_6 - Three-dimensional pentahedral element, with six nodes located at the six geometric corners of the pentahedron.
  • HEXA_8 - Three-dimensional hexahedral element, with eight nodes located at the eight geometric corners of the hexahedron.
