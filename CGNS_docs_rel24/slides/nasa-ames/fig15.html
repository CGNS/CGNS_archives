This diagram shows the structure of a BCDataSet_t node and its children.

At the top is the BCDataSet_t node. Below BCDataSet_t are the following nodes.

  • DataClass_t (data class)
  • Descriptor_t (text)
  • DimensionalUnits_t (units)
  • GridLocation_t (grid location)
  • IndexArray_t (PointList)
  • IndexRange_t (PointRange)
  • ReferenceState_t
  • UserDefinedData_t
  • BCData_t (DirichletData)
  • BCData_t (NeumannData)

Below one of the BCData_t nodes are the following nodes.

  • DataArray_t (data)
  • DataClass_t (data class)
  • Descriptor_t (text)
  • DimensionalUnits_t (units)
  • UserDefinedData_t
