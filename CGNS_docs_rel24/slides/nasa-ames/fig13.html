This diagram shows the structure of a GridCoordinates_t node and its children.

At the top is the GridCoordinates_t node. Below GridCoordinates_t are the following nodes.

  • DataArray_t (grid coordinates)
  • DataClass_t (data class)
  • Descriptor_t (text)
  • DimensionalUnits_t (units)
  • Rind_t (number rind planes)
  • UserDefinedData_t
