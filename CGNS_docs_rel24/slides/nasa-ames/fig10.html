This diagram shows the structure of a Zone_t node and its children.

At the top is the Zone_t node. Below Zone_t are the following nodes.

  • ArbitraryGridMotion_t (motion type)
  • ConvergenceHistory_t (number iterations)
  • DataClass_t (data class)
  • Descriptor_t (text)
  • DiscreteData_t
  • DimensionalUnits_t (units)
  • Elements_t (element type)
  • FamilyName_t (family name)
  • FlowEquationSet_t
  • FlowSolution_t (model type)
  • GridCoordinates_t (name)
  • IntegralData_t
  • Ordinal_t (ordinal number)
  • ReferenceState_t
  • RigidGridMotion_t (motion type)
  • RotatingCoordinates_t
  • UserDefinedData_t
  • ZoneBC_t
  • ZoneGridConnectivity_t
  • ZoneIterativeData_t
  • ZoneType_t (zone type)
