This default Export panel has the title "My Conversion", and the text "Convert Command" at the top, followed by four text input boxes. From top to bottom these are labeled "Executable", "Options", "CGNS Input", and "CGNS Output". All but "Options" have "Browse" buttons to their right. In this example the first, third, and fourth boxes contain the text "convert_cgns", "CGNSInput", and "CGNSOutput". The "Options" box is empty.

At the bottom are "Accept" and "Cancel" buttons.
