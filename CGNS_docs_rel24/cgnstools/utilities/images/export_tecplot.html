The ADFviewer Tecplot export window, titled "Tecplot Export", has three sections, from top to bottom.

First is the "CGNS Input" section, with three text input boxes, labeled "Filename", "Base Index", and "Solution Index". At the right of the first box is a "Browse" button, and at the right of each of the other two boxes is a check box labeled "default".

Next is the "Tecplot Output" section, with a single text input box labeled "Filename". To the right of the box is a "Browse" button.

The last section is titled "Options". It has three check boxes, labeled "ASCII Tecplot Output", "Grid Output Only (no solution)", and "Use Volume Weighting".

At the bottom of the window are "Accept" and "Cancel" buttons.
