This diagram shows the structure of a UserDefinedData_t node and its children.

At the top is the UserDefinedData_t node. Below UserDefinedData_t are the following nodes.

  • DataArray_t (data values)
  • DataClass_t (data class)
  • Descriptor_t (text)
  • DimensionalUnits_t (units)
  • FamilyName_t (family name)
  • GridLocation_t (grid location)
  • Ordinal_t (ordinal number)
  • IndexArray_t (PointList)
  • IndexRange_t (PointRange)
  • UserDefinedData_t
