This diagram shows the top-level structure of a CGNS database.

At the top is the root node of the ADF file. Below that are the nodes CGNSLibraryVersion_t (containing the CGNS version number), and CGNSBase_t (containing the physical and cell dimensions), the root node of the CGNS database.

Below CGNSBase_t are the following nodes.

  • Axisymmetry_t
  • BaseIterativeData_t (number of steps)
  • DataClass_t (data class)
  • Descriptor_t (text)
  • DimensionalUnits_t (base units)
  • Family_t (family name)
  • FlowEquationSet_t
  • ConvergenceHistory_t (number of iterations)
  • Gravity_t
  • IntegralData_t
  • ReferenceState_t
  • RotatingCoordinates_t
  • SimulationType_t (simulation type)
  • UserDefinedData_t
  • Zone_t (vertex and cell sizes)
